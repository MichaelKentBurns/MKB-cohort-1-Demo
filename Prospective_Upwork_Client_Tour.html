Contact Survey

The first feature we'd like you to explore is the 'Contact' survey:

  • Open the survey page.
  • Fill out the form with your details and comments.
  • When asked "Would you like us to contact you?", select an option.
  • Press the submit button.

JSON Survey

In this field, Upon submission, the survey page queries the backend for a list of available surveys. When you select a survey, the questions are retrieved from the backend and then displayed in the form. After pressing the 'Submission' button, the page will generate a JSON file with your results, which is displayed on the page for demonstration purposes. You can download this JSON file and it will also be submitted to our backend where it is stored in the database.
